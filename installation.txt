1. Move "csgo_colormod" to this folder: Steam\SteamApps\Common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\csgo\resource
2. Add -language colormod in CS:GO launch options
3. Restart CS:GO & enjoy

1. 将 "csgo_colormod "移动到CSGO目录下的 \csgo\resource 
2. 在 CS:GO 启动选项中添加 -language colormod
3. 重新启动CS:GO

Not working?
1. Make sure that csgo_colormod is placed in the right folder (it needs to be inside the "resource" folder)
2. Check your launch options for spelling mistakes, this example will work: -novid -language colormod -tickrate 128

无法正常使用？
1. 确保 csgo_colormod 被放在正确的文件夹中（它需要在 "resource "文件夹中）。
2. 检查你的启动选项是否有拼写错误，例如： -novid -language colormod -tickrate 128
